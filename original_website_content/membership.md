# IC-EMS Membership

### Apply to IC-EMS at IU!
Become a member of IC-EMS at IU! Volunteer your time to EMS and First-Aid and make an impact on your community.

#### Member Benefits
- **Direct Patient Care Experience:** Work alongside IU Student Health Center Nurses and IU Health LifeLine EMS providers at events like Little 500.
- **Clinical Volunteer Hours:** Gain hands-on experience in patient care.
- **Life-saving Skills:** Practice the BLS scope of practice with frequent skills and scenario training.
- **Connection with IU School of Medicine:** Network at the IU Interprofessional Simulation Center.
- **Leadership Opportunities:** Roles available in finance, training, public relations, and more.
- **Expansive Trainings:** Learn from physicians and clinicians across various specialties.
